Welcome to SafEra, an innovative solution developed by Team CARMAA. SafEra is designed to address the critical issue of women's safety by leveraging advanced analytics and real-time monitoring. The app detects potential threats and sends alerts, helping to create safer environments for women and aiding law enforcement in preventing incidents.

SafEra includes functionalities such as:
i. Person detection with gender classification
ii. Gender distribution analysis
iii. Lone woman identification at night
iv. Detection of women surrounded by men
v. Gesture-based SOS recognition
vi. Hotspot identification based on past alerts

The increasing incidents of crimes against women necessitate the use of real-time surveillance and analytical solutions. Our mission is to provide a system that can detect potential threats and respond in real-time by notifying users and emergency contacts. SafEra offers comprehensive safety features:
i. Real-time Location Tracking: Monitors users' locations and alerts them of high-risk areas.
ii. Gesture-based Alerts: Immediate alerts triggered through simple gestures.
iii. Voice and Video Evidence Collection: Automatically captures evidence during critical situations.
iv. Aadhaar OTP Verification and Secure Authentication: Ensures privacy and accurate gender detection.
v. Survey and Hotspot Detection: Identifies and alerts users of unsafe areas.

Challenges and Issues
Some challenges we face include:
i. Data Privacy and Security: Safeguarding user information with encryption and secure authentication.
ii. Accuracy of Detection: Continuous refinement of gender detection, location identification, and crowd density analysis.
iii. Misuse Prevention: Incident reviews and reporting mechanisms to prevent false alerts and maintain system integrity.

SafEra by Team CARMAA offers a tech-driven approach to women's safety. By combining real-time monitoring, advanced threat detection, and immediate emergency responses, SafEra empowers women to navigate the world safely and confidently. This application represents a critical step toward building safer communities.
